BE THE ARCHITECT OF YOUR LIFE

Create real habits through scientifically proven methods

Habitect is built on habit psychology and healthy methods to guide you on the path to great habits and systems.

HOW YOU WILL DO IT

1. ENTER FUTURE HABITS

Start by filling in your goals into your app. Here you can choose frequency, type of habit, reminders and more to fit your daily life.

2. CHECK OFF GOALS

On the homepage you can see what today's habits will be. You can then check them off when you have completed them!

3. SEE YOUR PROGRESS

After some time your statistics page will fill up with useful and insightful information about your progress in general, per habit and much more.

Follow your progress as you grow using our statistics page.

As you're making progress it's important to know how far you've come and how great you're doing. That's why Habitect has an amazing statistics page with many stats, but still easy to use and clear!

You can for example see your Heatmap Calendar, Habitification of a habit, general and per habit statistics and so much more!

WHY IT WORKS

Attractive, effortless, evident and satisfying

These four things are crucial to forming new habits. Our app is well-designed and optimized to make your experience as smooth as possible throughout your journey, with no unnessecary clutter in the way.

Being able to keep track of your habits and seeing your progress through the statistics makes your experience using Habitect satisfying and your habits evident.

Systems are everything, that is why Habitect is here

When trying to form your new habits, don't focus on goals. Rather focus on systems and your goals will follow.

That is why Habitect revolves around forming systems and making you consistent in your habits, your goals can be a motivator but you shouldn't rely on only your goals to keep you going.
