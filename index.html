TREE OF HABITS

The perfect method for reaching your biggest goals in life

First you write down your Life Goals, as big as they can possibly get. Then think about what goals you would need to reach or what skills you would need to possess to be able to reach your life goals. These are you Sub Goals.

Then think, what would I need to do daily to reach my primary goals. These are your Daily Goals. This way your biggest dreams get broken down into small, attainable and managable pieces. It’s the most effective and true way of creating new habits and a better life for yourself.
